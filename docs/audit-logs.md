# Audit Logs

Audit Logs provide a detailed record of activities within your account, helping you track changes and ensure security.

## Viewing Audit Logs

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Audit Logs**.
2. You will see a chronological list of actions performed by team members.

## Logged Events

The audit log captures the following information for each event:
- **User**: Who performed the action.
- **Action**: What happened (e.g., "Created Link", "Updated Note", "Deleted User").
- **Resource**: The specific item affected.
- **Date & Time**: When the action occurred.
- **IP Address**: The IP address from where the request originated.

## Filtering

You can filter logs by:
- **User**
- **Date Range**
- **Action Type**
