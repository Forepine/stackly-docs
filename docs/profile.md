# Profile Settings

Manage your personal account details, including your profile picture and display name.

## Updating Your Profile

1. Log in to your Stackly dashboard.
2. Click on your **Avatar** in the top right corner and select **Profile**.
3. **Profile Picture**: Click on the camera icon to upload a new image.
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG.
   - Max size: 2MB.
4. **Display Name**: Edit the **Name** field to change how your name appears to your team.
5. Click **Save Changes**.
