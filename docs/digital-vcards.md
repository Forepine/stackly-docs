# Digital vCards

Create stunning, interactive digital business cards that make networking effortless. Share your contact information, social media profiles, and custom links in one beautiful, mobile-friendly page.

## What is a Digital vCard?

A Digital vCard (also known as Bio Page) is your modern business card on the web. Instead of exchanging physical cards, share a single link that contains all your professional information. Visitors can save your contact details directly to their phone with one tap, and you get real-time analytics on who's viewing your card.

## FAQ

### Can I have multiple vCards?
Yes! Create unlimited vCards for different purposes - personal, business, events, etc.

### Can visitors save my contact to their phone?
Absolutely! Your vCard includes a "Save to Contacts" button that works on all devices.

### Do I need to pay to remove the watermark?
The default "Stackly" watermark can be removed with a premium plan, or you can add your own branding logo.

### Can I use my own domain?
Yes, custom domains are available as a premium feature.

### Is there a limit on custom links?
No, you can add unlimited custom links to your vCard.

### Can I see who viewed my vCard?
You can see analytics including view counts, geographic data, and device types. Individual visitor identification depends on your privacy settings and plan.

### What happens when I reach the click limit?
You can increase or remove click limits at any time. Premium plans offer unlimited views.
