# Team Members

Stackly allows you to collaborate with your team by adding members to your account. You can manage their roles, permissions, and access status directly from the dashboard.

## Managing Team Members

### âž• Invite New Members

To add a new team member, you need to send them an invitation.

1. Navigate to **Team Members**.
2. Click **Add User**.
3. Enter the user's **Name** and **Email Address**.
4. Select a **Role** (Admin, Manager or Viewer).
5. Click **Send Invitation**.

The user will receive an email with an invitation link containing a secure token to join your team.

> [!NOTE]
> You can only invite users if your current plan has available user slots. If you have reached your limit, you will need to upgrade your subscription.

## Roles & Permissions

Stackly supports role-based access control to ensure security.

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to all features, billing, and team management. Cannot be deactivated or removed by others. |
| **Admin** | Can invite new users, manage team members, and access most features. |
| **Manager** | Can invite new users, manage team members, and access most features. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to resources. Cannot make changes to team settings or invite users. |
