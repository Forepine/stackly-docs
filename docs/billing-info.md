# Billing Information

Manage your tax details and billing preferences.

## Tax Information

Ensure your invoices are compliant by providing your tax details.

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**.
2. Locate the **Tax Info** section.
3. Select your **Tax Type** (e.g., VAT, GST).
4. Enter your **Tax Number**.
5. Click **Save**.

## Invoices

You can view and download your past invoices from the **Invoice History** section on the same page.
