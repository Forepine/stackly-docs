# Integrations

Stackly integrates seamlessly with your favorite communication tools, **Slack** and **Discord**, to keep your team in the loop. Receive real-time notifications about team activity directly in your preferred channels.

## Supported Integrations

### <img src="https://cdn.worldvectorlogo.com/logos/slack-new-logo.svg" width="20" style={{marginRight: '8px', verticalAlign: 'middle'}} /> Slack
Connect your Slack workspace to receive instant updates. You can choose specific channels for different types of notifications to ensure the right people get the right information.

### <img src="https://cdn.worldvectorlogo.com/logos/discord-6.svg" width="20" style={{marginRight: '8px', verticalAlign: 'middle'}} /> Discord
Integrate with your Discord server to broadcast team activities. Configure webhooks to send messages to dedicated text channels.

## Setup Guide

For a detailed step-by-step guide and to connect your accounts, please visit our [Integrations Page](https://www.stackly.in/integrations).

1. Navigate to the **Integrations** section in your Stackly dashboard.
2. Select the service you want to connect (**Slack** or **Discord**).
3. Click **Connect** and authorize Stackly to access your workspace/server.
4. Select the **Channel** where you want to receive notifications.
5. Toggle the specific **Events** you want to be notified about.
6. Click **Save** to activate the integration.
